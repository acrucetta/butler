# 2026-02-20

## Training plan to remember

3-Day 5/3/1 for Strength, Hypertrophy, and Trail Readiness

- Day A (Squat):
  - Squat 5/3/1 + FSL 5x5
  - Pull-ups 4-6 sets
  - Bulgarian split squats 3x10/leg
  - KB swings 100 total (24-32kg)
- Day B (Press, alternate bench/OHP weekly):
  - Bench or OHP 5/3/1 + FSL 5x5
  - Other press light 3x8
  - Rows 4x10
  - Face pulls 3x20
  - Turkish get-up 5/side (16-20kg)
- Day C (Deadlift):
  - Deadlift 5/3/1 + FSL 3x5
  - Pull-ups 3-4 sets
  - Rows 3x10
  - Ab wheel 3x8
  - Farmer carries 5-6 heavy trips

Rules:
- After each session: 5 min mobility (ankles, hips, t-spine)
- On hiking/running weeks: cut or skip KB finisher
- Swings: 100 total, any set structure with quality reps
- TGUs: 5 per side, slow and controlled

Accessory load preference and ranges (pounds):
- Bulgarian split squats: 20-30 lb/hand to start; progress to 45-65 lb/hand
- Rows: 45-75 lb/hand
- Face pulls: 20-40 lb
- Pull-ups: bodyweight first, then weighted
